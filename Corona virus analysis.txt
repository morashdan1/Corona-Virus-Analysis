The COVID-19 pandemic has highlighted the importance of data-driven insights in understanding its spread. 
As a data analyst, I analyzed a COVID-19 dataset to uncover valuable insights. By conducting a thorough analysis, 
we aimed to identify patterns and trends to understand virus transmission better, ultimately aiding the fight against the pandemic and protecting public health.

Dataset Description: 

Province: Geographic subdivision within a country/region.
Country_Region: Geographic entity where data is recorded.
Latitude: North-south position on Earth's surface.
Longitude: East-west position on Earth's surface.
Date: Recorded date of CORONA VIRUS data.
Confirmed: Number of diagnosed CORONA VIRUS cases.
Deaths: Number of CORONA VIRUS-related deaths.
Recovered: Number of recovered CORONA VIRUS cases

Insights:
COVID-19 Pandemic duration: January 22, 2020, to June 13, 2021.
India has the highest number of recovered cases.
Samoa, Kiribati, Dominica, and the Marshall Islands have the lowest death counts.
The US leads in confirmed COVID-19 cases.
Peak confirmed cases occurred in April 2021.
Peak death rate in January 2021.
